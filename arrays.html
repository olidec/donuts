Question (a) What is the result of a.length?

The result of a.length is 5.

Question (b) What are the entries of a after the command a.push('hello')?

The entries of a after the command a.push('hello') are [2, 4, 6, 8, 11, 'hello'].

Question (c) What are the entries of a after the command a.pop()?

The entries of a after the command a.pop() are [2, 4, 6, 8, 11].

Question (d) What is the output of a.join(' ** ') ?

The output of a.join(' ** ') is "2 ** 4 ** 6 ** 8 ** 11".

Question (e) What is the output of a.join()?

The output of a.join() is "2,4,6,8,11".

Question (f) What is the output of a.slice(1,3)?

The output of a.slice(1,3) is [4, 6].
